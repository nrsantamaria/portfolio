About Me

Born in Southern California, raised on the island of Oahu, went to college in San Francisco, living in Oregon. I like to roam...

I am always hungry to grow and learn new things, so I took the risk to make the career switch from a project coordinator at a conference planning company to web developer. While working as a project coordinator and collaborating with our web developer, I gained experience creating web based products, and serving the clients that used them. I effectively communicated with clients to help them translate loose ideas into content our web developer could visualize. It was incredibly fulfilling, working through bugs and developing unique innovative planning systems my previous employer continues to use. Wanting to expand my technical skills beyond the planning phase, I started at Epicodus in their Ruby, JavaScript and Rails track.

Having dedicated four months of full time coding, I am confident in my ability to develop Ruby on Rails products as well as work with JavaScript, including Angular.js, on the front-end. Additionally, the most important skill I have gained from this program is the ability to take something completely foreign and in a short time become proficient in it.
